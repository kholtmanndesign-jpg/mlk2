Who Was He? His Dream Why He Matters

Who Was Him?

Martin Luther King Jr. was a kind and courageous man who worked hard to make the world a fairer place. He beleived that everryone—no matter their skin colr—should be treeted with respect.

He became a leader because he spoke up when things were unfair, even when it was difficult. Many people followed him because they trusted him and knew he wanted to help others.

Fun Fact: MLK became the youngest person to win the Nobel Peace Prize at the time!

His Dream

Martin Luther King Jr. shared a powerful dreem: a world where all children and grow together as friends. He believed that kindness and peaceful actions could change unfair rules.

His famous “I Have a Dream” speech inspired millions of people to work together for fairness.

Why He Matters

MLK showed that one person with a strong voice and a caring hart can make a big difference. He taught people too solve problems without fighting and to stand up for what is right.

Today, we remember him as a hero who helped make ur country better and more fair.
